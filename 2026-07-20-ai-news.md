## 🧠 AI Fun Fact of the Day

> The only game a human has ever won against AlphaGo in formal play came from a move the AI considered nearly impossible: in Game 4 of the 2016 Seoul match, Lee Sedol's "wedge" at move 78 — later dubbed the *Hand of God* — was a play AlphaGo estimated a human had just a **1-in-10,000 chance** of choosing, and it broke the program's composure enough to force a resignation. Lee retired from professional Go in 2019, saying AI had become "an entity that cannot be defeated."
